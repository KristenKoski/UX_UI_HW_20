Presentation Manor Senior Living

This redesign was a collaborative effort between three designers. We undertook the task of redesigning the website of Presentation Manor Senior Living, a cherished non-profit committed to caring for the elderly and proving a welcoming space they can call home. Throughout our collaboration, we recognized the importance of designing a website that spoke to Presentation Manor’s core values and allowed prospective residents and their families to easily locate information

User Interviews

We interviewed a diverse range of individuals aged 40-75. Participants are researching senior living communities for themselves or their aging parents. They require a thorough understanding of floor plans, amenities, services, and pricing transparency to inform their decision

Our Findings

Stakeholder Interviews

It was important to our stakeholders that we address the outdated website layout to provide better navigation

Additionally, they needed their website to meet accessibility standards in order to provide equal access to all users

User Interviews

Pricing transparency is important to most users and many expressed that existing senior living website’s rarely advertise their pricing models

Virtual tours and digital presence are highly valued by users, as they provide comprehensive information and facilitate communication

Define

Meet Zoey

A 50-year-old design professional who is searching for a suitable retirement home for her elderly parents

Goal: Find a senior residence home that meets her parent's needs for amenities, care options, and overall quality of life

Pain-Point: Finds it frustrating to book tours and believes that senior living websites do not have enough information available online

Problem Statement


The Presentation Manor Senior Living Website was designed with the goal of providing easy access to information related to services, amenities, pricing, room availability, and detailed descriptions of facilities and programs for prospective residents and their families across all age demographics

However, we have observed that our website is not meeting these objectives, our users are struggling to access comprehensive information and detailed descriptions of amenities, services, and pricing offered by Presentation Manor. The lack of accessible information is resulting in infrequent bookings and a hesitancy among prospective residents in applying to Presentation Manor

How can we improve our website to address these issues and achieve key success metrics such as increasing booked tours to showcase a robust digital presence, boosting user visits, and fostering greater family involvement and connectivity?

Ideate

We delved into structuring the website to enhance user interactions. Our aim was to craft an intuitive digital environment that users could seamlessly navigate

Homepage Iterations

Usability testing revealed that the amount of content on the homepage was overwhelming and a few users suggested that we adjust the hierarchy of the ‘Book a Tour’ button to emphasize its importance


We “hid” excess content by showcase only essential information pertaining to the care options offered. We introduced a 'care type quiz' to provide visitors with the flexibility of delving deeper into each service

We refined the 'Book a Tour' button, enhancing its visibility and underlining its significance to users. We opted to rename it 'Schedule Tour', to better align with the scheduling functionality offered
